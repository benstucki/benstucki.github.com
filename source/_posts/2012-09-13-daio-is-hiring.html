What is Daio?

Daio (Design Assisted I/O) is building the future of interaction design software. Why are we prototyping mobile apps with software that’s meant to make swimsuit models glisten? Why are we still sending design files back and forth as attachments? Daio believes that mockups & prototypes are the best form of communication for interactive projects, but competitors like Basecamp, Trello, Fogbugz and others don’t do them justice. We’ll be working on a SaaS product for teams of designers, developers, and others to collaborate on interactive projects. We can do better, and we’re going to – with your help.
