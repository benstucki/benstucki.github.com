For the second week in my Month of Flex, I've created another completely free and open source component for Flex 2. The reflection component is used to give the illusion of a reflective surface in your Flex 2 applications. It includes a few properties for custom fading, skewing and scaling and was made with real-time reflection in mind. This component is a little different from similar work like Trey Long's or PixelFumes' because its not used like a filter or effect and takes up its own screen real estate. Stay tuned next week for another free Flex 2 component.
