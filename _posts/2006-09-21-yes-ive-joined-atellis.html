As you might have read on Chafic's blog, I have officially joined Atellis and now find myself just outside the Washington, DC area. I'm already deep into some really awesome Flex and .NET work and sharpening my skills with a few of the best Flash, Flex and .NET experts in the industry. Once I get settled in a bit more I'll get back into regular blogging and code samples as well. Also, we're still looking for more developers. If you like developing cool stuff and want to innovate, I fully recommend contacting Chafic about our open positions.
