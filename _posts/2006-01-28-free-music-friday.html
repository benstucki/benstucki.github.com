In my last post I gave source to an ActionScript 3 class used to display ID3 APIC images in Flex 2. In it, I used a few MP3 files from a friend's former band as examples and thought you might like to know where you can download some more. Take a quick trip over to imaginarybaseball.com and visit their shows page. You'll see three albums worth of great music from under-appreciated Nashville talent. It's easy for good music to get overlooked in this area, so I wanted to take a moment and thank them.
